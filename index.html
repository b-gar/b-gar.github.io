Data Science Enthusiast

Up until 2018 my intentions were to be a strength and conditioning coach because I enjoyed working out, sports, and valued health. I picked up a minor in Business Administration towards the end of my Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology program because I found business intriguing, a useful minor, and I had eligibility still remaining in athletics. This is where I realized my passion and skills in IT while taking a required IT class.

After graduating with my bachelor's degree and completing that IT course, I realized I was interested in a career in technology. My professor in that IT class pointed me to a master's program which I applied for and completed in August 2020. The summer of 2018 I stumbled across a sports analytics conference (thanks dad), called Great Lakes in Analytics in Sports Conference. This is where I realized how interested in data science I was. And I guess you could say the rest is history.
